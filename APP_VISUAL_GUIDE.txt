═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                              COLOR LEGEND
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

▓▓▓  Green (#2E7D32)      - Primary color (AppBar, active tabs)
🟠   Orange (#FF6F00)     - Accent color (FAB)
🔵   Blue (#1976D2)       - Info messages
🟢   Green                - Success, Positive trends, Healthy status
🔴   Red                  - Error, Negative trends, Logout
🟡   Yellow/Orange        - Warning
⚪   White                - Cards, backgrounds
⚫   Gray                 - Inactive elements, text
